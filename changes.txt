# STOCKPULSE AI – UPDATED PRODUCTION LOGIC (ACCURACY IMPROVEMENTS)

This document outlines all required changes to upgrade your current system into a more accurate, production-grade scoring engine.

---

1. MACHINE LEARNING IMPROVEMENTS (HIGHEST PRIORITY)

---

❌ CURRENT:

* Target: Next-day price prediction
* Data: 6 months per stock
* Model: Random Forest

✅ CHANGE TO:

1.1 Target Variable:

* Replace next-day prediction with 5-day return classification:

  y =
  1  if return_5d > +2%
  0  if return_5d < -2%
  ignore otherwise

→ This reduces noise and improves accuracy.

---

1.2 Dataset Size:

* Use minimum 2–5 years of historical data
* OR train on multiple stocks together

---

1.3 Model Upgrade:

* Replace Random Forest with:

  * XGBoost OR LightGBM

---

1.4 Add Features:

* Returns: 1d, 3d, 5d, 10d
* Volatility (rolling std)
* RSI
* MACD
* Bollinger Band width
* Volume z-score
* ADX (trend strength)
* Market trend (Nifty return)

---

1.5 Probability Calibration:

* Apply calibration (Platt scaling or isotonic regression)
* Output must be true probability (0–1)

---

1.6 ML Confidence:

* Calculate entropy:

  confidence_ml = 1 - [ -p*log(p) - (1-p)*log(1-p) ]

---

2. TECHNICAL INDICATOR SYSTEM (MAJOR CHANGE)

---

❌ CURRENT:

* Binary signals (bullish/bearish)
* Simple counting

✅ CHANGE TO:

2.1 Convert Indicators to Continuous Scores:

* Normalize each indicator using z-score:

  z = (value - mean) / std

* Convert to probability:

  p = 1 / (1 + exp(-z))

---

2.2 Direction Adjustments:

* RSI:
  p_rsi = 1 - sigmoid(z_rsi)

* Bollinger Bands:
  p_bb = 1 - sigmoid(z_bb)

* Trend indicators (MACD, MA):
  p_trend = sigmoid(z_trend)

---

2.3 Combine Indicators:

S_tech = Σ (weight_i * p_i)

Suggested weights:

* Trend: 0.4
* Momentum: 0.2
* Volatility: 0.2
* Volume/ADX: 0.2

---

2.4 Technical Confidence:

confidence_tech = 1 - variance(p_i)

---

3. SENTIMENT ANALYSIS UPGRADE

---

❌ CURRENT:

* TextBlob (weak accuracy)

✅ CHANGE TO:

3.1 Replace TextBlob with:

* FinBERT (preferred)
* OR VADER (fallback)

---

3.2 Sentiment Calculation:

For each headline:
score_j ∈ [-1, 1]

Apply weights:
weight_j = exp(-lambda * time_decay) * source_weight

Final sentiment:
S_sent = weighted average

Convert to probability:
p_sent = (S_sent + 1) / 2

---

3.3 Sentiment Confidence:

* Based on:

  * variance of scores
  * number of headlines

---

4. REMOVE DANGEROUS OVERRIDE

---

❌ REMOVE:

* 70% crash → force Strong Buy

---

✅ REPLACE WITH SAFE VERSION:

If:

* price drop > 50% (6 months)
* RSI < 30
* volume spike

Then:

* Add small boost (+0.1) to score
* DO NOT override system

---

5. ADD MARKET CONTEXT (IMPORTANT)

---

Add:

* Nifty trend score
* Sector trend score

Compute:

market_score = 0.5 * nifty + 0.5 * sector

Apply adjustment:

final_score *= (0.7 + 0.3 * market_score)

---

6. DYNAMIC WEIGHTING (KEY IMPROVEMENT)

---

❌ CURRENT:

* Fixed weights (40/35/25)

---

✅ CHANGE TO:

Compute effective weights:

w_eff = (base_weight * confidence) / total

Base weights:

* ML: 0.5
* Technical: 0.3
* Sentiment: 0.2

---

This ensures:

* High-confidence signals get more weight
* Weak signals get reduced influence

---

7. FINAL SCORING FORMULA

---

Compute:

Final Score =
(ML * ML_confidence * 0.5) +
(Technical * 0.3) +
(Sentiment * 0.2)

Apply dynamic normalization.

---

Then:

Score = clamp(0–1) * 100

---

---

Score ≥ 45 → Hold
Score ≥ 30 → Sell
Score < 30 → Strong Sell

---

Also display:

Confidence Score (0–100)
